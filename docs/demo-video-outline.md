# Demo Video Outline

Target length: 2 to 3 minutes.

## 0:00 - 0:20 Problem

Show the opening application and say:

> Local merchants need green checkout campaigns that behave like trustworthy financial products, not private promises. Green Impact Voucher turns a customer payment into escrowed, verified impact on Stellar.

## 0:20 - 0:45 Product

Show the three top modes:

- Customer Checkout
- Merchant Console
- Verifier Vault

Say:

> The app connects checkout, a smart contract vault, verification, and customer receipts in one transaction flow.

## 0:45 - 1:15 Customer Checkout

Show the checkout panel:

- Voucher quantity
- Voucher price
- Impact funded
- Escrow status
- Buy Voucher

Say:

> A customer buys a Solar Classroom Voucher for 0.10 XLM. This funds 10 kWh of verified solar energy and moves payment into the contract vault.

## 1:15 - 1:40 Impact Receipt

Show the Impact Receipt panel:

- Buyer
- Campaign
- Impact
- Paid
- Transaction
- Verification status

Say:

> The customer receives receipt-grade proof with the campaign, impact amount, payment amount, transaction reference, and verification state.

## 1:40 - 2:10 Stellar Proof

Open Stellar Expert and show:

- Contract page
- Buy voucher transaction
- Withdraw funds transaction

Say:

> The buy transaction moves funds into the contract vault. The withdraw transaction releases funds only after verification.

## 2:10 - 2:40 Verifier and Vault

Return to the app and show:

- Verify Impact
- Withdraw Vault Funds
- Verification coverage
- Retired verified impact

Say:

> A project owner or admin verifies delivered impact with a report hash. After verification, the merchant or project owner can withdraw verified funds from the vault.

## 2:40 - 3:00 Close

Say:

> Green Impact Voucher is a green checkout finance application on Stellar. It makes small merchant payments transparent, verifiable, and useful for customers, merchants, and project owners.
